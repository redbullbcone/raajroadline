our history

The foundation stone of Raaj Roadlines was laid by Mr. Rajinder Singh Gill in 1992 with the vision of providing a complete in-time and right transportation solution that is efficient and reliable. Since then we have represented continuous growth year on year. We become the Best Transport Company in Mumbai.


We have been awarded the best transport company in Mumbai by RCF. We are serving this Industry since 1992 with an experience of 40 years in the transportation industry and continuously winning clients both in the Public & Private sectors. We proudly call ourselves specialists in the sector of Bulk transportation of tankers, hazardous chemicals, and gases products & specialty Chemicals. With our state-of-the-art infrastructure, systems & controls being in sync we come across as a quality supplier with economical viability for our clients.
